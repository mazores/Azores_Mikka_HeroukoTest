Eco-Friendly

A single Sinaya Cup can be used throughout your menstrual cycles for two years, diverting hundreds of non-biodegradable toxic plastic napkin wastes from our landfills and environment.

Comfortable

Unlike bulky pads and drying tampons, the Sinaya Cup can't be felt once properly inserted. No more annoying blood clots, plastic-induced sweat and stained skin from prolonged napkin usage.

Waterproof

Ideal for use in water activities such as swimming, rafting, diving, surfing. Can also be used in rigorous activities and sports such as biking, trekking and mountain-climbing. Rain-proof, flood-proof and sweat-proof.

Cost-Effective

Without having to buy napkins and tampons each month, you can cut your menstrual product cost by at least a half. For each cup we sell, we will donate cups to underprivileged communities.
